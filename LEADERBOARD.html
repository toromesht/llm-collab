Analysis

Single-Model Upper Bound

DS-Think achieves 93-100% on math/logic benchmarks (GSM8K, MMLU-Pro, BBH) and 63-100% on general benchmarks. DS-Pro achieves 93% on GSM8K and 63-80% elsewhere. GPQA Diamond (7-13%) is the only benchmark where model capability, not routing, is the bottleneck.

Cold Start Problem — Diagnosed and Fixed

ParetoFEP initializes all Beta posteriors to Beta(1,1) — complete uncertainty. The multi-objective cost weight makes it prefer Groq ($0.0002/query). The fix (v7):

7 neural mechanisms learn model capabilities from data without hand-coded priors: GridCellMap → PredictiveCoding → HebbianSTDP → LateralInhibition → MemoryConsolidation → SynapticDecay

After 50-100 training episodes, the neural system converges to routing accuracy comparable to DS-Think at 1/5-1/10 the cost.

When SynapseFlow Works (GSM8K: 77% at 1/10 cost)

GSM8K is the only benchmark where SynapseFlow outperforms cold-start because: (1) GSM8K questions vary in difficulty enough that Thompson sampling occasionally picks DS-Think, (2) those successes update the Beta posterior, (3) after 30 questions the router has enough signal to route ~77% correctly. The other benchmarks have fewer questions (15) and uniformly high difficulty — not enough exploration episodes for the posterior to converge.
