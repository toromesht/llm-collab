2. 脑干层 — HD计算 + SDM

2.1 超维编码 (Kanerva 2009, §3)

HV = sign( Σᵢ fᵢ · (2·Bᵢ - 1) ) ∈ {-1,+1}¹⁰⁰⁰⁰

其中: D=22维特征, Bᵢ∈{0,1}¹⁰⁰⁰⁰随机基超向量
fᵢ∈[0,∞) 特征分数, sign(x)=+1 if x≥0 else -1

Kanerva, P. "Hyperdimensional Computing." Cognitive Computation 1:139-159 (2009).

2.2 SDM读取 (Kanerva 1988, Ch.4.3)

dⱼ = popcnt(HV XOR Aⱼ) (汉明距离)
激活集 = {j : dⱼ < 500} (半径=5%HV_SIZE)
输出 = mean(Cⱼ for j∈激活集) → best_region = argmax

Kanerva, P. "Sparse Distributed Memory." MIT Press (1988).

2.3 SDM训练 (Kanerva 1988, Ch.4.4) — Hebbian更新

C(i,j) ← C(i,j) + α·(1-C(i,j)) if i=correct_region
C(i,j) ← C(i,j) - α·0.1·C(i,j) if i≠correct_region
其中 α=0.1 (学习率)

2.4 难度评分 (Huang et al. ACL 2024)

complexity = Σ wᵢ·featuresᵢ
difficulty = 1/(1+exp(-complexity/5.0))

Huang et al. "Harder Tasks Need More Experts: Dynamic Routing in MoE Models." ACL 2024, arXiv:2403.07652.

4. M1-M2: STDP + BCM

M1: STDP (Song, Miller & Abbott 2000)

Δw = A⁺·exp(-Δt/τ⁺) (LTP: 正确→增强)
Δw = -A⁻·exp(Δt/τ⁻) (LTD: 错误→减弱)
其中 A⁺=0.15, τ⁺=5, A⁻=0.10, τ⁻=3

Song, Miller & Abbott. Nature Neuroscience 3:919-926 (2000). Eq.2.

M2: BCM (Bienenstock, Cooper & Munro 1982)

θ_M = E[y²] (滑动阈值)
dw/dt = η·y·(y-θ_M)·x
y>θ_M → LTP, y<θ_M → LTD

Bienenstock et al. J Neurosci 2(1):32-48 (1982). Eq.7.

5. M3: L-LTP 通路硬化 (Kandel 2001)

硬化电位 S(t) = Σᵢ A_LTP · exp(-(t-tᵢ)/τ_h)
A_LTP=0.15, τ_h=50轮, θ_hardening=0.5
触发: S(t)>0.5 AND use_count≥3

Kandel. Science 294:1030-1038 (2001). Nobel Prize.
cAMP/PKA/CREB→蛋白质合成→树突棘结构永久增大.

6. M4: 表现驱动LTD (Bienenstock 1982 + Dudek 1992)

主要 (表现驱动): Δw_correct=+0.05, Δw_wrong=-0.15
次要 (时间衰减): w(t)=w₀·exp(-Δt_wall/τ_forget)
τ_forget=720h (30天), τ_max=60天, θ_prune=0.02
修剪: w<θ_prune AND (fail_count≥8 OR idle>60d)

Bienenstock (1982) + Dudek & Bear. PNAS 89(10):4363-4367 (1992).

7. M5: 元可塑性快捷键 (Abraham & Bear 1996)

f_window = count(1h内使用次数) / 1h
触发: f>5/h AND use_count≥3
匹配: cos_sim(features, pattern) > 0.85 → 跳过脑干

Abraham & Bear. TINS 19(4):126-130 (1996).

8. M6: 突触标记与捕获 (Frey & Morris 1997)

PRP生成: 通路硬化时释放 A_tag=0.05 PRPs到区域池
PRP衰减: d[PRP]/dt = -[PRP]/τ_prp, τ_prp=100
标记状态: w>0.25 AND recent_uses≥2
捕获: 标记通路消耗PRP → Δw=min(pool, 0.08)

Frey & Morris. Nature 385:533-536 (1997). Fig.3.

9. M7: 簇状可塑性 (Fu et al. 2012)

同区域内cos_sim≥0.3的通路 → 共强化
Δw_neighbor = 0.03·cos_sim
生物: 同一树突分支上相邻突触共享PRPs

Fu et al. Nature 483:92-96 (2012). Fig.2.

10. M8: 资格迹 (Gerstner et al. 2018)

Eligibility更新: de/dt = -e/τ_e + spike (Eq.2)
τ_e=3.0, spike=0.5 when pathway used
转换: if e>0.4 AND correct: Δw=0.1·e (Eq.3)
e重置为0

Gerstner et al. Nat Neurosci 21:1273-1283 (2018). Box 2.

11. M9: 神经调质 (Fuxe et al. 2007)

多巴胺(DA): DA←DA+α·(actual_reward-expected_reward)
DA调制学习率: effective_Δw = Δw·(0.5+DA)
血清素(5-HT): 5-HT←5-HT+(1/τ₅)·(reward-5-HT)
5-HT>0.6 → 探索模式: 尝试次要模型

Fuxe et al. Prog Neurobiol 83:263-287 (2007). Sec.3.
Schultz et al. Science 275:1593-1599 (1997) — dopamine RPE.

12. M10: 星形胶质细胞Ca²⁺ (Perea & Araque 2007)

τ_ca·d[Ca]/dt = -[Ca] + Σ(activity_i) + noise
τ_ca=3600s (1h), γ_ca=0.2, θ_ca=0.5
调制: w_effective = w · (1+γ·tanh([Ca]-θ))

Perea & Araque. Science 317:1083-1086 (2007). Fig.3.
Haydon & Carmignoto. Physiol Rev 86:1009-1031 (2006).

13. M11: 临界态 (Beggs & Plenz 2003)

分支比: σ = mean(cascade_size) / mean(cascade_size-1)
σ≈1: 临界态, 信息传输最大化
σ<1: 亚临界(死网络), σ>1: 超临界(爆炸)
自调: θ_prune += η·(σ-1), θ_harden -= η·(σ-1)
η=0.01, ε=0.1

Beggs & Plenz. J Neurosci 23(35):11167-11177 (2003). Fig.3.

14. SPSA 在线参数优化 (Spall 1992)

θ_{k+1} = θ_k - a_k·(J⁺-J⁻)/(2c_k·Δ_k)
a_k = a/(A+k)^0.602, c_k = c/k^0.101
Δ_k ∈ {±1}^d (Bernoulli扰动)
J=1(正确), J=0(错误)

Spall. IEEE Trans Automatic Control 37(3):332-341 (1992).

15. TDA 拓扑缓存 (Ballester 2024)

特征空间中余弦相似度匹配:
sim = dot(f₁,f₂)/(|f₁|·|f₂|)
if sim>0.7: 复用缓存的路由决策
缓存大小: 200条, 7天过期

Ballester et al. "TDA for Neural Network Analysis." arXiv:2312.05840 (2024).
